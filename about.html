About Us

Founded in 2018, Fardin’s Kitchen began with a mission to bring the authentic taste of Bangladeshi cuisine to every home and event. From humble beginnings, we have grown into one of Dhaka’s most beloved catering and food service brands.

We specialize in traditional dishes like Kacchi Biryani, Mutton Rezala, Shorshe Ilish, and many more. Our chefs use fresh ingredients, age-old recipes, and a whole lot of love to bring you unforgettable meals.
